About

Email: zhaoyichang@csu.edu.cn
ORCID: 0000-0002-9651-4457

Research activities focus on antimicrobial pharmacology, infectious disease epidemiology, and clinical pharmacokinetics. Current work emphasizes optimization of antimicrobial therapy, characterization of PK/PD variability, and identification of clinical and environmental determinants of antimicrobial resistance.

Quantitative methodologies include meta-analysis, population pharmacokinetic (PopPK) modeling, real-world evidence analysis, and causal inference to support individualized dosing and evidence-based antimicrobial stewardship.

Published research includes multiple first-author articles in international peer-reviewed journals across pharmacology, infectious diseases, and environmental health.
